Safty Guide

Travellers are treated well and Sri Lanka is a safe place to visit for tourists. It is a country where you are unlikely to encounter violence, but you should be careful when it comes to petty crime because its rate is high. Any non-nationals of Sri Lanka will need a visa to enter the country. The type of visa required is subject to your proposed length of stay the purpose of your visit.
